# PN Junction Diode

## Definition
A PN junction diode is formed when p-type and n-type semiconductors are joined together.

## Formation of Depletion Region
When the junction is formed, electrons from n-region diffuse to p-region and holes from p-region move to n-region.

This creates a depletion region near the junction.

## Working Principle
- Forward bias → current flows  
- Reverse bias → current blocked  

## Applications
- Rectifier
- Detector
- Switching circuit
